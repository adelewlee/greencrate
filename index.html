Welcome to Greencrate

Recipes, ingredients, and instructions for healthy meals sent straight to your door.

All for only $8 per meal.

Register and Order

After registering, you'll let us know which days you want a food kit, and at approximately what time (in between 4-7pm). In addition, you'll let us know what cutlery and cooking materials you do and don't have, so we can tailor our meals to be accessible to you! If you are lacking some core materials, we can send you some for free on your first kit.

Assemble and Prepare

On our end, we bulk purchase healthy and affordable foods from farmers, lowering costs for both us and you! From our menu designs, we create food kits for each day, containing ingredients for our recipe of the day, and a recipe card with detailed instructions on how to create a beautiful, awesome meal!

Transport and Deliver

We conveniently deliver the food kit to your house or place of residence, of which your time and day you can choose. We eliminate the time and resource investment needed to head to several grocery and specialty stores, as well as auxiliary transporation costs. In addition, the kits are reusable, so once you're done we'll pick up your leftover boxes!

Cook and Enjoy

After receiving the kit, you can cook each recipe (with the aforementioned included recipe cards) in less than 30 minutes and using only a few basic materials. Hopefully, you learn some pretty rocking awesome cooking skills! Once you're done, you'll enjoy a pretty awesome meal!
